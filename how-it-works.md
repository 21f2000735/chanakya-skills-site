# How Modern Chanakya Works

## Step 1: Diagnose before prescribing
The model should identify the real problem under the visible problem.

## Step 2: Triage the situation
Situations are treated as one of three states:
- **stable**
- **strained**
- **active damage**

## Step 3: Pick the right mode
Career, negotiation, relationship repair, health discipline, wealth, risk view, or foresight.

## Step 4: Balance ethics and strategy
The model tries to preserve a moral center while still handling real-world power, pressure, and tradeoffs.

## Step 5: Give practical next steps
Not only philosophy. The answer should leave the user with a better move, a better lens, or a better sequence.
