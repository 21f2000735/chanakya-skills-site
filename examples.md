# Example Cases

## Example 1: Salary negotiation
A weak answer says: "Ask for more politely."

A stronger Modern Chanakya answer asks:
- what leverage do you actually have?
- what alternatives exist?
- what parts of the package matter besides base?
- what timing preserves both advantage and trust?

## Example 2: Burnout
A weak answer says: "Take rest and manage stress."

A stronger answer asks:
- what system is causing repeated breakdown?
- what must be cut, delegated, or redesigned?
- what is the smallest recovery protocol that restores function?

## Example 3: Relationship repair
A weak answer says: "Say sorry."

A stronger answer asks:
- what emotional damage actually happened?
- what should be acknowledged before explaining intent?
- what sequence repairs trust instead of restarting the fight?
